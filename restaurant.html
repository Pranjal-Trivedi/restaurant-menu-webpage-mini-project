Food Menu

1. Green Goddess Garden Salad with Whole Grain Brown Bread

Description: A revitalizing green vegetable salad consisting of crisp mixed greens, cucumber slices, avocado chunks, edamame beans, and toasted pumpkin seeds, served with a side of whole grain brown bread. Tossed in a light herb-infused dressing for a burst of freshness.

Nutrient Content: Healthy Fats: Avocado offers heart-healthy monounsaturated fats, beneficial for overall well-being. Protein: Edamame beans contribute plant-based protein, aiding in muscle maintenance and providing essential amino acids. Vitamins and Minerals: Various greens and cucumber provide vitamins (K, A, C) and minerals (potassium, folate) necessary for optimal health.


Price: ₹ 250

2. Nutty Oat and Mixed Nut Salad

Description: A nutritious blend of oats, mixed nuts (such as almonds, walnuts, and pecans), dried cranberries, and mixed greens tossed in a tangy vinaigrette dressing. This salad offers a delightful combination of textures and flavors.

Nutrient Content: Whole Grains: Oats provide complex carbohydrates, dietary fiber, and various vitamins and minerals like iron and magnesium. Healthy Fats: Nuts offer heart-healthy fats (omega-3 fatty acids) and protein, aiding in maintaining overall health. Antioxidants: Dried cranberries contribute antioxidants, promoting cellular health and immune function.

Price: ₹ 350

3.Garden Fresh Veggie Salad

Description: A colorful medley of mixed greens, bell peppers, carrots, broccoli florets, and sunflower seeds tossed in a light balsamic vinaigrette. This salad offers a refreshing crunch and a variety of textures.

Nutrient Content: Vitamins and Minerals: Various vegetables contribute to a wide range of vitamins (A, C, K) and minerals (potassium, magnesium) necessary for overall health. Antioxidants: Found in brightly colored vegetables, aiding in reducing inflammation and supporting cellular health. These salads offer a diverse range of nutrients and flavors, catering to different dietary preferences and providing a healthy and delicious dining experience.

Price: ₹ 250

4.Quinoa Power Salad

Description: A hearty salad combining cooked quinoa, diced cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and fresh herbs with a zesty lemon vinaigrette. This salad provides a satisfying texture and a burst of flavors.

Nutrient Content: Protein: Quinoa is a complete protein source, ideal for muscle repair and growth. Fiber: Promotes digestive health and helps maintain steady blood sugar levels.

Price: ₹ 250

5.Roasted Red Pepper and White Bean Dip with Crackers

Description: A flavorful and creamy dip made from roasted red peppers, white beans, garlic, and a blend of Mediterranean herbs and spices. Served alongside whole-grain crackers for a delightful crunch.

Nutrient Content: Protein: White beans are a good source of plant-based protein, aiding in muscle repair and providing satiety. Fiber: Both white beans and whole-grain crackers contribute to a fiber-rich snack, promoting digestive health. Antioxidants: Roasted red peppers contain antioxidants like vitamin C and beta-carotene, supporting immune function and skin health.

Price: ₹ 250

6. Tropical Paradise Salad

Description: A delightful mix of pineapple chunks, mango slices, shredded coconut, and a drizzle of honey-lime dressing. This salad offers a tropical escape with a perfect balance of tangy and sweet flavors.

Nutrient Content: Vitamin A: Present in mangoes, supports vision and immune function. Bromelain: Found in pineapple, aids digestion and reduces inflammation.

Price: ₹ 550
